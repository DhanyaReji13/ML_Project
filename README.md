## Business Problem
Accurate sales forecasting is critical for retail businesses to optimize pricing, promotion planning, and inventory management. Poor forecasts can lead to revenue loss, excess inventory, or stock shortages.

## Objective
To forecast daily store sales and analyze the impact of promotions, holidays, and seasonality using historical retail data.

## Approach
- Cleaned and validated historical sales and store-level data
- Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify trends and seasonality
- Engineered features such as promotions, holidays, and store characteristics
- Built regression-based models for sales forecasting
- Evaluated model performance and interpreted results from a business perspective

## Key Insights
- Promotions had a strong positive impact on daily sales
- Sales exhibited clear weekly and seasonal patterns
- Holiday periods influenced sales variability
- Store type and competition distance affected overall performance

## Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn)
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Feature Engineering
- Regression Analysis
- Model Evaluation

## Outcome
This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow focused on generating business insights to support pricing and inventory decisions.
